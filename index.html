About me

Turning surveillance data into decision-ready intelligence.

My work focuses on health emergency information management, outbreak analytics, interoperable surveillance systems, and capacity building for public health decision-making.

Public health emergency analytics

I coordinate and support health information management activities for regional emergencies, including outbreak dashboards, situation reports, statistical outputs, and decision briefs.

Surveillance systems strengthening

I provide technical guidance on interoperable surveillance systems, integrated disease surveillance, case-based reporting, and real-time disease monitoring in complex settings.

Applied data science for response

I use R, Power BI, DHIS2, Excel, and epidemiological tools to build automated analytics pipelines, outbreak line-list databases, dashboards, and reproducible reports.

About the course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity

8-week guided course

The programme covers practical AI use for websites, documents, data analysis, literature review, text analysis, chatbots, task automation, scientific communication, and a capstone showcase.

  • Weekly live workshops
  • Live help sessions every week
  • Private community forums
  • Personalized feedback on assessments
  • Capstone project coaching
  • Shareable certificate
